About Me

Hello, World! My name is Nick Pool and I am a web developer with experience in both Python and MEAN stacks. I am infinitely curious and love finding and solving problems. Software development has allowed me to merge my passion for learning and helping others into one discipline.

In my free time I enjoy camping, paddling my kayak, and homebrewing.
